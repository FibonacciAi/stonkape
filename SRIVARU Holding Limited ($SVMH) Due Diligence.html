Executive Summary

SRIVARU Holding Limited ($SVMH) is an innovative electric vehicle (EV) company based in India, specializing in the design and manufacture of high-performance electric motorcycles. With a mission to revolutionize the Indian two-wheeler market, SRIVARU aims to provide sustainable, efficient, and technologically advanced electric motorcycles that cater to the needs of modern commuters.

Company Overview

Founded

2018

Headquarters

Founder & CEO

Market Focus

Electric Motorcycles

SRIVARU Holding Limited is committed to accelerating the adoption of electric vehicles in India by offering affordable and high-quality electric motorcycles. The company's flagship product, the PRANA electric motorcycle, combines performance, technology, and sustainability.

SRIVARU Holding Limited represents a promising entrant in the Indian electric two-wheeler market. Its focus on high-performance electric motorcycles at affordable prices addresses key consumer needs. While challenges exist, particularly in terms of competition and market acceptance, the company's strategic positioning offers promising prospects.
